Welcome to JDBot

The simple easy to use bot that provides a wide range of entertainment and utility for your server. Originally meant to be a test bot, this soon grew, and now it's at 90+ servers and verified! This bot is meant to be having all of the features, an "all in one" bot.

Entertainment commands!
Hug, pat, kiss, all the interactive commands. These are powered by the asuna and some random api. (yes it's actual name)
Utility commands!
Extremely detailed analysis of user info and server info! This uses the BetterUserConverter and the BetterMemberConverter!
Moderation commands!
The classic warn, kick, and ban. Warning someone will cause the bot to DM the person warned.
